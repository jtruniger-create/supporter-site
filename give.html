Partner with us

Thank you for considering partnering with us financially. Financial partners allow us to stay present in ministry but they also get to join in what God is doing in Spain.

Give

We are excited to partner with CTEN (Commission to Every Nation). CTEN is a recognized 501(c)(3) — gifts are tax-deductible. Donations are solicited with the understanding that CTEN maintains complete discretion and control over the use of all donated funds. CTEN honors donor preferences as long as it is within its stated tax exempt purposes. Please contact the donor care team with any questions.

Donor care: trinettezirkel@cten.org, lindaperkins@cten.org, robinhill@cten.org
