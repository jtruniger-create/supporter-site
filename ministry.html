Meeting pilgrims exactly where the path takes them

Photo: Camino trail
or pilgrim outreach moment

Puntos de Luz runs points of welcome along the Camino — places where pilgrims, many of them walking through real grief, searching, or simple exhaustion, can stop, rest, and be met with genuine care.

Our teammates Seth & Crystal Grotzke are already living and serving in Galicia, and we'll be joining their work directly, learning the rhythms of the Camino Francés and building out Caldas de Reis as a stop of its own.

The goal isn't a transaction at a table. It's a conversation, a place to sit down, and — for those who want it — a door to something lasting after the walk ends.
