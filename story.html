From Minneapolis to the Way of St. James

We've lived in Minneapolis, built a life there, and never expected it to lead to a stone path across northern Spain. But over the past year, the call to Galicia has become impossible to ignore — and impossible to walk alone.

In fall 2026 we'll relocate to Caldas de Reis, one of the last towns pilgrims pass through before Santiago de Compostela, to serve alongside Puntos de Luz. We're sent out by Hope Community Church and partnered with CTEN for the logistics of getting there and staying there well.

This page is where we'll keep you close to that story as it unfolds — not just the ask, but the actual life of it.
