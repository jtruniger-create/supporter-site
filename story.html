From Minneapolis to Spain

We have been married since September of 2025 and living in Minneapolis. We love it there but believe God is calling us to take a step towards Spain.

In early 2027 we'll move to Caldas de Reis for a two year commitment. Caldas is one of the last major towns that pilgrims pass through before Santiago de Compostela. We are going to be joining with an existing church planting team and involved in Camino and local ministry.
